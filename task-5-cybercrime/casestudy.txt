Phishing is a common cybercrime where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords or banking details.

In a typical phishing attack, the victim receives a fake email that appears to be from a trusted organization like a bank. The email contains a link that redirects the user to a fake website. When the user enters their details, the attacker steals the information.

Students are often targeted due to lack of awareness. The consequences can include financial loss and identity theft.

This case highlights the importance of being cautious while using the internet and verifying all online communications.
